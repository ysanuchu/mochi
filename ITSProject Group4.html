ABOUT US

Mochi Desu is a new shop that is willing to share or advertise the traditional japanese rice cake and also mochi ice cream. In Japan, it's a symbol of good fortune that was widely consumed by the aristocracy. It represents sustenance for those that need it, as it's incredibly filling. More than that, it's something that we enjoy even today as a treat that many people love. Although the variations of mochi is really broad but we mainly focus on the traditional japanese mochis. Mochi is similar to dango, but is made by pounding grains of rice, while dango is made with rice flour.
